serving a 25-year sentence, Vincent “The King” Morello returns to a New York City he barely recognizes. His once-feared crime family has been dismantled, his territories seized by a coalition of Russian mobsters and a corrupt real estate mogul. Refusing a quiet retirement, Vincent assembles a small crew of loyal old-timers and disillusioned young outsiders. He doesn’t just want his empire back; he wants to reshape the rules of the game. Using a blend of old-school brutality and surprising strategic cunning, he plays the rival factions against each other.
